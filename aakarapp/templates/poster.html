ABOUT US

Aakaar IIT Bombay is proud to launch its Eighth International Civil Engineering Symposium (ICES)in collaboration with American Society of Civil Engineering (ASCE) Indian Western Region with the prime motive of encouraging young research enthusiasts in the field of civil engineering to engage in vigorous research and take civil engineering to a whole new level. ICES, IIT Bombay, will act as a platform to exchange ideas and scientific knowledge through intense professional interaction among aspiring civil engineering researchers who are inclined toward discovering, learning and innovating new domains. We believe this will lead to the fruitful germination of collaborative research work. We look forward to bringing together and supporting such talented and enthusiastic people.

TECHNICAL POSTER PRESENTATION

The Technical Poster Presentation is a way to communicate your research and your understanding of the topic in a concise format. It requires analyzing, evaluating, and synthesizing the idea and creatively demonstrating the findings of your research work in a visually capturing way. If you are a research enthusiast and feel that you can make an impression with your extraordinary demonstrating skills then the Department of Civil Engineering invites you to participate in Technical Poster Presentation 2024.
