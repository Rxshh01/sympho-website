ABOUT US

Aakaar IIT Bombay presents its Seventh International Civil Engineering Symposium (ICES) which will act as a platform to exchange ideas and scientific knowledge through intense professional interaction among aspiring civil engineering researchers who are inclined toward discovering, learning and innovating new domains. We believe this will lead to the fruitful germination of collaborative research work. So, we present to you the first edition of the 3-minute Thesis Talk for enthusiastic researchers to show off their speaking skills.

What do you have to do?

Curate your thesis, such that you are able to express it in 3 minutes and present it in such a way that a non-specialist can understand.
“Present Technical Jargons In Non Technical way”

Why Participate?

Develop your skills

Know how effectively you can explain your research to a non-specialist audience. Being able to present your work in front of the world is a skill not everyone possesses

Mentorship

Get to know all the tips and tricks from the winners* themselves on how they achieved what they have achieved and learn from their experience & mistakes

Networking Opportunities

You get a chance to build your network with academicians from IITs, industry leaders, and also you may be pitching in front of your stakeholders

FREE workshop

All the 3MTT participants will get to attend a workshop on “Academic Writing” for free

Prizes

Get certification and prizes worth INR 25,000

Resume point

Participating and winning an exclusive IIT-NIT 3MTT competition would be a great add-on to your resume
